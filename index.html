Kerala Hill Station Experience

Get a taste of authentic Kerala hill life at Thushara Homestay! Live in an independent 1BHK cottage with AC, enjoy traditional meals from Vanitha Hotel, and explore the scenic beauty of the Western Ghats.

The stay includes:

View Gallery
  • Independent 1BHK with AC, living room, kitchenette and private bathroom
  • Comfortable beds (we can accommodate maximum of 2 adults)
  • Traditional Kerala meals from Vanitha Hotel (attached women's group restaurant)
  • Free parking in safe residential area
  • Easy access to all hill attractions

If you would like to visit Paithalmala Hill Station or take a Palakkayam Thattu tour during your stay at Thushara Homestay, we can help arrange transportation. Please ensure to plan your hill station visits by checking weather conditions. Our location provides easy access to multiple attractions in the Western Ghats region, making it an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty and cultural richness of Kerala's hill country. The homestay is strategically positioned to offer both comfort and convenience for travelers seeking an authentic mountain experience.
