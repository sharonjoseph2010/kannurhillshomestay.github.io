Thushara Homestay

Experience the authentic Kerala hills

Get a taste of authentic Kerala hill life! Stay in our comfortable 1BHK homestay, enjoy fresh meals from Vanitha Hotel (women-run restaurant attached to our homestay), and explore the stunning Western Ghats from your doorstep.

Your stay includes:

  • Independent 1BHK with AC, living room, kitchenette and private bathroom
  • Comfortable accommodation for up to 2 adults
  • Traditional Kerala meals available from Vanitha Hotel (women's group restaurant) attached to homestay
  • Free parking in safe, prime residential area
  • Local guidance for hill station exploration
  • Easy access to all major Kannur hill attractions
