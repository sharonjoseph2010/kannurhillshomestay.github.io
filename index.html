Hill Stay

Get a taste of authentic Kerala hill life! Book yourself a Hill Stay at Thushara Homestay! Live in an independent 1BHK cottage with AC, enjoy traditional meals from Vanitha Hotel (women's group restaurant), explore scenic attractions, and soak in the beauty of the Western Ghats!

The stay includes:

  • Independent 1BHK with AC, living room, kitchenette and private bathroom
  • Comfortable beds (we can accommodate maximum of 2 adults)
  • Traditional Kerala meals from Vanitha Hotel (attached women's group restaurant)
  • Free parking in safe residential area
  • Easy access to all hill attractions
